How to Build an Effective Skincare Routine

Building a skincare routine doesn't need to be complicated. With the right steps and products, you can create a daily ritual that keeps your skin clean, hydrated, and glowing.

Step 1: Cleanse

Start with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil. Garnier's Micellar Water is a great choice for all skin types.

Step 2: Tone

Use a toner to refresh your skin and tighten pores. Look for alcohol-free formulas to avoid irritation.

Step 3: Moisturize

Moisturizing is essential to lock in hydration. Use a lightweight moisturizer in the morning and a richer cream at night.

Step 4: Protect

Always apply sunscreen during the day, even if it’s cloudy. SPF protects your skin from premature aging and damage.

Consistency is key. Follow this routine daily to see long-term benefits and maintain healthy skin with Garnier’s natural solutions.
